Who am I?

Hi! I'm Pablo, a Spanish data scientist living in Rome (Italy).

Since my childhood, I have always liked maths and physics. After school, I opted for a Bachelor’s and a Master’s degree in Aerospace Engineering in Madrid.

When doing my thesis in the Department of Mathematics, I discovered what Machine Learning was and all its potential. Since then, I have always worked as a Data Scientist from Rome. I will not be exhaustive here, as you can have a look at my LinkedIn profile for further details. Here is a summary:

  • I did an internship in a consulting company.
  • I worked for G-Evolution, a telematics startup, for three years
  • I tried to develop an app, Lingua Ignis, where users could learn languages from contents chosen by themselves and connect with people with similar interests. The idea didn’t work, but I learnt a lot from that experience.
  • Last year, I joined Houseplus, a small Proptech startup in a very early stage, where I have developed and managed any system related to data.
  • Very soon, I will work part-time as a teacher in a course of Data Analytics.

Having worked for small startups and lacking a direct supervisor for most of my career, I am used to learning on my own, managing projects and cooperating with other team members with different roles.

Over time, I am becoming interested in Data Engineering and in general Computer Science.

I also like economics and humanities. In particular, I love learning (and teaching) languages. I have worked as a text translator, have taught Spanish and I volunteer weekly teaching Italian to refugees in my local parish.
